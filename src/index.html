About Palette Studio

A single-page tool for miniature painters: pick or explore a colour and get colour harmonies matched to real paints you can buy — with cross-brand equivalents, colour-blindness simulation and WCAG contrast checks.

How the matching works — and why it's honest

Colours are converted sRGB → CIELAB (D65) and matched by CIEDE2000 (ΔE 2000); harmonies rotate hue in HSL. That maths is exact and unit-tested. But paint hex values are approximate (most are community-sourced and tagged “approx”), so every match shows its ΔE and a plain-language quality label — “ideal vs actual” always surfaces the gap and never implies a perfect match.
